## Project Overview

CareerForge AI is a multi-agent AI-powered career intelligence platform designed to help students become industry-ready professionals.

The platform analyzes resumes, identifies skill gaps, recommends jobs and internships, generates personalized career roadmaps, conducts AI-powered mock interviews, and provides future skill recommendations.

By combining multiple career development services into a single platform, CareerForge AI enables students to make informed career decisions and improve their employability.

---

## Problem Statement

Millions of students struggle during placement preparation due to the lack of personalized guidance.

Common challenges include:

- Poorly optimized resumes
- Lack of awareness about industry requirements
- Difficulty identifying skill gaps
- Limited interview preparation
- Lack of personalized career guidance
- Difficulty finding suitable jobs and internships

These challenges reduce employability and increase the time required for placement preparation.

---

## Proposed Solution

CareerForge AI addresses these challenges through a multi-agent architecture that provides end-to-end career assistance.

The platform allows students to upload their resumes and receive:

- Resume Analysis
- ATS Optimization Suggestions
- Skill Extraction
- Skill Gap Analysis
- Job Matching Recommendations
- AI-Powered Mock Interviews
- Career Roadmap Generation
- Future Skill Recommendations
- Cover Letter Generation

The platform acts as a personalized AI career mentor.
